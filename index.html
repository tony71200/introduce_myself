About me

I’m an AI Engineer who loves turning research into things people actually use. With a Master’s in Computer Science from National Cheng Kung University (NCKU), Taiwan. I specialize in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, computer vision and industrial automation

I focus on optimizing Large Language Models (LLMs) and computer vision systems for edge devices and industrial platforms—aiming for the sweet spot of accuracy, scalability, and computational efficiency.

I’m originally from Danang city and earned my Bachelor’s degree at Danang University of Science and Technology. During my master’s, I worked as a graduate researcher at the Robotics Lab, NCKU, contributing to medical imaging projects such as lung CT dataset curation, tumor detection models, and 3D reconstruction for analysis.

Beyond engineering, I see AI as both tool and craft—something that can connect with design, creativity, and everyday life. That mindset keeps me building, experimenting, and sharing. I’m always open to collaborations, research ideas, and meaningful products. Feel free to reach out, explore my GitHub, or download my Resume.

What I'm Doing

  • AI & Machine Learning

    I design end-to-end ML and Computer Vision systems—from clean data pipelines and model training to evaluation and deployment on edge and servers. Recent work expands into LLMs/NLP (retrieval, lightweight fine-tuning, agent workflows) to connect vision and language. The goal: efficient, reliable solutions that ship to production with clear metrics.

  • Embedded Systems

    I build real-time, AI-enabled embedded software that integrates cameras, sensors, and hardware for robotics and IoT. I care about low latency, robust I/O, and maintainable code that runs well on constrained devices.

  • Research & Development

    I turn ideas into working prototypes fast—experiment, measure, iterate. Interests include intelligent systems, robotics, and deep learning, with a focus on methods that transfer to production and are backed by clear ablations.
